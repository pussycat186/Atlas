üîè Cosign Signatures & Verification

All artifacts are signed using Cosign with keyless OIDC (GitHub Actions).

Verify SBOM Signature

cosign verify-blob \
  --bundle sbom-bundle.json \
  --certificate-identity-regexp="https://github.com/pussycat186/Atlas/*" \
  --certificate-oidc-issuer=https://token.actions.githubusercontent.com \
  atlas-ecosystem-sbom.json
